Our Programmes

We offer three free programmes to empower youth and fight climate change in South African townships.

Tree Planting Initiative

We plant indigenous trees in schools, churches, and public spaces. Each tree planting event includes a workshop on why trees matter for fighting climate change, preventing soil erosion, and providing shade.

Learn More →

Recycling Workshops

We teach young people how to turn waste into reusable products like plant pots, furniture, and school supplies. Some participants have started small recycling businesses.

Learn More →

Youth Climate Camps

Weekend camps where young people learn about climate science, environmental advocacy, and green job skills. Camp graduates become Eco-Champions in their schools.

Learn More →

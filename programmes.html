Our Programmes

Three ways we take climate action in townships

Tree Planting Initiative

We plant indigenous trees in schools, churches, and public spaces. Each tree planting event includes a workshop on why trees matter for fighting climate change, preventing soil erosion, and providing shade.

Impact so far: 5,000+ trees planted in 8 townships.

Recycling & Upcycling Workshops

We teach young people how to turn waste (plastic bottles, old tyres, cardboard) into reusable products like plant pots, furniture, and school supplies. Some participants have started small recycling businesses.

Impact so far: 200 youth trained, 5 micro-enterprises started.

Youth Climate Camps

Weekend camps where young people learn about climate science, environmental advocacy, and green job skills. Camp graduates become Eco-Champions in their schools and communities.

Impact so far: 3 camps held, 120 youth graduated as Eco-Champions.

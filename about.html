About GreenFuture SA

Our Story

GreenFuture SA was founded in 2021 by three environmental science students at the University of KwaZulu-Natal. We started with a single tree-planting event in Chesterville township, where 50 young people showed up on a Saturday morning to plant 200 trees.

Today, we operate in 8 townships across Durban and Pietermaritzburg, have planted over 5,000 trees, and trained more than 300 youth in environmental leadership and green skills.

Our Mission

To empower youth in South African townships to take climate action through environmental education, tree planting, and recycling initiatives.

Our Vision

A South Africa where every township community is green, clean, and climate-resilient.

Our Core Values

  • Youth-led: Young people are at the centre of everything we do.
  • Community-first: We work with, not for, township communities.
  • Sustainability: Our projects are designed to last.
  • Transparency: We are accountable to our donors and volunteers.
